            <p>
                Julie Stout is a graduate student originally from Illinois working towards a dual library science and folklore degree at Indiana University. She is interested in the intersection between heritage traditions, oral narrative, cultural memory, and archives & records management. She seeks to understand how these concepts may be addressed through the lens of public folklore and digital content & curation practices.
            </p>

            <p>
                This collection was made as the final project for her master's degree under the direction of the Department of Folklore and Ethnomusicology faculty, Drs. Jon Kay, Jason Jackson, and Barbara Hillers, and Associate Director and Head of the Indiana University Archives and Special Collections, Carrie Schwier. Thank you to all those who supported and provided guidance throughout the entirety of this project, from its inception to fruition!
            </p>
